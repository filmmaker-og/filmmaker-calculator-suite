3. **`5_Waterfall`**: Change cell C4 (Total Gross Revenue) to model any revenue scenario instantly
4. **`8_Sensitivity`**: The heat maps use static scenario math — if you want them to reflect your exact Assumptions inputs, you can swap the hardcoded values with formulas referencing `2_Assumptions`
